Bronco Ember

By Derek Mata
By Derek Mata

Bronco Ember

  • www.autonomousobservation1.nasatechleap.org Winners Announcement

Description:

Published: November 10, 2021 favorite0 forum0 poll44
By: Derek Mata, Cal Poly Pomona
Category: Engineering
Hashtags: #AI #ai_devlopment #computervision #ControlSytems #Design #EmbeddedSoftware #EmbeddedSystem #Engineering #firmware #Linux #Nasa #NvidiaJetson #pcbdesign #Project #Robotics #Teamwork #techleap

A CubeSat device which utilizes computer vision and a SWIR camera to detect wildfires. Once the camera detects a fire, the device determines the fire’s exact geographical location with the assistance of GPS and triangulation. The device will then log this information and alert the proper authorities regarding the fire. This project requires a deep knowledge in embedded hardware, embedded software, computer vision, and robotics.
