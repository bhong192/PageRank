    Affordable and Excellent: CPP College of Engineering

    CPP College of Engineering's one of the largest, top-ranking and affordable engineering colleges in the nation. With 11 undergraduate and 7 graduate programs to choose from, we get you hands-on with industry-standard hardware, software and practices. You'll be ahead of peers in other colleges who operate in the theoretical but rarely in the practical. We graduate over 1,200 Bronco Engineers every year. They go onto solve hard problems and invent new solutionsbe one of them.

    11 Undergraduate Programs to Start Your Career

    7 Graduate Programs to Advance Your Career

    Our engineering programs are top-ranking nationally among 220 engineering programs in the U.S. News & World Report 2022:

    No. 5 Best Undergraduate Engineering Program*
    No. 3 in Computer Engineering
    No. 4 in Civil Engineering
    No. 4 in Electrical Engineering
    No. 9 Mechanical Engineering

    *Among public universities where a doctorate is not offered
