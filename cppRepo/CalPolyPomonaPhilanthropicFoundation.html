    THRIVING PHILANTHROPY, SUCCESSFUL STUDENTS

    At Cal Poly Pomona, we not only offer our students an exceptional polytechnic experience, we also provide as many resources as possible to help them succeed. We rely on a strong foundation of supporters like you to ensure that success.

    The Cal Poly Pomona Philanthropic Foundation serves as the leader for the universitys fund-raising efforts, continuing to build on our achievements. By broadening partnerships and deepening financial support, the foundation will strengthen the university's educational mission by increasing student scholarships, faculty research initiatives and projects that will benefit the economic vitality of the state and region.
