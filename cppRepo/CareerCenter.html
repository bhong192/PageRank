COVID-19 UPDATE  

  

CAREER CENTER SERVICES

Effective March 18 through the end of spring semester: Cal Poly Pomona will transition to virtual/remote instruction for all classes. Employees who can work remotely should not come to campus, and critical business functions will continue.  Currently the Career Center staff is working remotely and the career center is closed.

 

EMPLOYERS

 

To our employers, please read our message regarding Engagement and Recruitment Opportunities during COVID-19.

 

 

ATTENTION STUDENTS AND ALUMNI

The Career Center has multiple handouts however, during this time, please take a few moments to review the "Before You Job Search" handout. There is valuable information for you to be aware of when job searching.

 

 

 We welcome you to explore the Career Center. If you find any accessibility issues or need accommodations utilizing registration or accommodation for any services, please contact career@cpp.edu or  call 909.869.2342 during regular office hours www.cpp.edu/~career/hours-and-location.shtml
