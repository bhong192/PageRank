    Faculty Career Development.

    CAFE actively supports faculty, tenure-line and lecturer, in all stages and facets of their careers at Cal Poly Pomona. We provide orientation for new faculty, pedagogical resources, collaborative opportunities, and confidential consultations.

    Pedagogy & Educational Technologies.

    CAFE offers pedagogical tools and technical support for faculty to create outstanding online, hybrid, and face-to-face courses. We provide consultation and support to staff and administrators in using educational technologies to meet departmental needs.
