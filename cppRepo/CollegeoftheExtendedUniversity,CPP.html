    Cal Poly English Language Institute (CPELI)

    • Intensive English Program  Six levels; Five 8- week terms per year
    • Customized programs combining English and university major of interest
    • Programs that increase confidence and ability in English for high school and university students
    • TOEFL/IELTS waiver to Cal Poly Pomona undergraduate and graduate programs
    • University application advising and cultural orientation for international students
    Visit CPELI
