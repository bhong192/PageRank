Employment

Please Read: Important information regarding COVID-19.
CSU requires faculty, staff, and students who are accessing campus facilities to be immunized against COVID-19 or declare a medical or religious exemption from doing so. Any candidates advanced in a currently open search process should be prepared to comply with this requirement. The systemwide policy can be found at COVID-19 Vaccination Interim Policy. For more information, visit the Safer Return website. Questions may be sent to asicovidsafety@cpp.edu.

Enhance the student experience. Your talents will establish opportunities for learning, leadership and development for students and its staff. You will help create an environment that promotes collaborative partnerships, cultural diversity and campus pride. Together we will foster student advocacy, representation, engagement and academic success. You will join an organization that provides high quality facilities, programs and services to students, administrators, faculty, staff, alumni and the off-campus community. Make a difference in lives, join ASI!

Welcome to Associated Students Incorporated, Cal Poly Pomona’s application process!

For a list of our current employment opportunities select either Student Job Opportunities and or Professional (Nonstudent) Job Openings employment category. Once on the Job Openings page, view full details of the job posting by selecting the job you are interested in and click “Apply” link to apply online. You can complete an application and upload your resume and cover letter. This application can be saved and used to apply for more than one job opening.

Online applications are stored on a secure site. Only authorized employees and hiring authorities have access to the information submitted.

It is important that your application show all the relevant education and experience you possess. Applications may be rejected if incomplete.

We welcome you to review our current job opportunities and encourage you to apply. If you are unable to complete the online application, contact Human Resources at asirecruitment@cpp.edu or 909-979-5546.

TO BE CONSIDERED FOR EMPLOYMENT:

  1. BOTH a cover letter and resume must be uploaded.
  2. After submitting an application you must proceed with completing your profile.

Student Job Openings

Thank you for your interest in working at Associated Students, Inc. (ASI). Student employment with ASI is open to all regularly enrolled students at Cal Poly Pomona who are at least 16 years of age, have the legal right to work in the United States, and maintain a Cal Poly Pomona and overall GPA of 2.00 or above.

View current job openings with:
ASI  |  ASI Campus Recreation  |  Handshake  |  Foundation

Professional (Non-student) Staff Job Openings

Associated Students, Inc. (ASI) seeks to hire creative, talented and motivated individuals who believe in our vision of “enhancing the student experience” by following our motto “Students Serving Students.” ASI is made up of student leaders, student staff, non-student staff, and a majority of ASI programs and services are run by students and guided by professional staff.

ASI  |  ASI Campus Recreation

Employment/Income Verification for Current and Previous Employees

ASI Cal Poly Pomona contracts with the Work Number for all verification of employment (VOE) and verification of income (VOI). Verifiers will have to pay a fee to verify current and or past employment or income. A verifier is a person or organization that needs to verify a borrower or applicant’s employment or income information. Only credentialed verifiers with a permissible purpose under the FCRA are allowed to access the income and employment information on The Work Number database.

If you are a current or former ASI employee and experience difficulty with The Work Number (TWN), we encourage you to contact TWN directly for further assistance by phone at 866-604-6572.

Website: www.theworknumber.com
Employer Code: 636183
Employer Name: ASI Cal Poly Pomona

  • 1st time verifiers, click on Sign Up > Verify for Your Organization
  • Returning verifiers, click on Log in > Verify for Your Organization

  • Notice of Availability – Annual Security Report and Annual Fire Safety Report (Clery Act)

    Pursuant to the Jeanne Clery Disclosure of Campus Security Policy and Campus Crime Statistics Act, the Annual Security Report (ASR) is available for viewing at https://www.cpp.edu/documents/annual_security_report.pdf. The ASR contains the current security and safety-related policy statements, emergency preparedness and evacuation information, crime prevention and Sexual Assault prevention information, and information about drug and alcohol prevention programming. The ASR also contains statistics of Clery crimes for Cal Poly Pomona for the previous three calendar years. A paper copy of the ASR is available upon request by contacting the Office of the Clery Director located at Student Services Building 121-Room 2511.

     

    Pursuant to the Higher Education Opportunity Act, the Annual Fire Safety Report (AFSR) is available for viewing at https://www.cpp.edu/housing/documents/fire_safety_report.pdf. The purpose of this report is to disclose statistics for fires that occurred within Cal Poly Pomona housing facilities for the previous three calendar years, and to distribute fire safety policies and procedures intended to promote safety on campus. A paper copy of the AFSR is available upon request by contacting the University Housing Services Office located at Building 74.

3801 West Temple Avenue, Pomona, CA 91768
