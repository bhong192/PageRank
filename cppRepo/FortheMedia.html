Welcome to Cal Poly Pomona's Online News Room!

Cal Poly Pomona is a rich resource for news — news about accomplishments and events, research and discovery, and opportunities and trends. We also have some of the region’s finest experts across a range of disciplines who can add perspective and depth to your reporting.
