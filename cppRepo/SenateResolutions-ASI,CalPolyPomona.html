Senate Resolutions

About Resolutions: The official expression of the opinion or will of a legislative body. The practice of submitting and voting on resolutions is a typical part of business in public assemblies.

How to suggest or co-write with ASI Student Government:

  1. Contact the Attorney General to discuss the topic at asiattygen@cpp.edu
  2. Join us at a ASI Rules & Policies Committee to edit and review the draft resolution
  3. If ASI Rules & Policies Committee approves, the draft resolution heads to the ASI Board of Directors for review. Be sure to be present in case there are questions
  4. Make any additional edits with the ASI Attorney General if needed or the ASI Board of Directors will vote to approve as is
  5. ASI will publicaly share the passing of the resolution via social media, email, website, etc.
