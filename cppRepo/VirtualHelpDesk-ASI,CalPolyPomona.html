Virtual Help Desk

Need help navigating ASI? No need to fear, our Virtual Help Desk is here! Drop in during our office hours for assistance with all things ASI.

Note: When you enter the Zoom office hours, you will be put in a waiting room until your hosts welcomes you into the space. You may use the chat, video, or audio features to communicate. Each person will be seen in the order in which they joined the waiting room. To ensure everyone is helped within a reasonable time, our ASI student representatives may need to follow up with you via your cpp.edu email address.
