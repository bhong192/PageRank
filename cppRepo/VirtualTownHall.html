    Virtual Town Hall

    Do you have questions about Safer Return, the vaccine requirement or weekly public health testing? Every Thursday from noon to 1 p.m. during fall semester, the Safer Return Task Force will host a Virtual Town Hall on Zoom for the campus community to hear whats happening and ask questions. All students, faculty and staff are invited to attend.   

    The meetings will be attended by several task force members, including CLASS adjunct faculty member Brian Newman (host) and Frances Teves (task force chair).
