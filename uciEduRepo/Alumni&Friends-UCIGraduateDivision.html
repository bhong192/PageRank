Graduate Alumni Chapter

The UCI Alumni Association is organized to enrich the lives of alumni and engage them in a lifelong advancement of UC Irvine. The Association seeks to create a community for alumni, foster school spirits, support today’s students and advocate on behalf of the university.

Industry Partnership Program

The Leadership Council Business & Industry Partnering Program is established to identify and pursue working relationships with UCI-area business and industry partners, with a focus on connecting UCI Graduate students

Mentorship Program

The Mentoring Program is focused on cultivating quality mentoring relationships is an essential component of career preparation for graduate students and postdoctoral scholars.
