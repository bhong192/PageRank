Details

The Conte Center @ UCI addresses how early-life experiences, and especially early-life adversity, influence brain maturation and contribute to vulnerability to mental health problems throughout life. The Conte Center presents a monthly seminar lecture series. The title of the talk will be "Alcohol & the Adolescent Brain" and will take place on November 23, 2021 from 1:00 - 2:15pm (PDT) on zoom.
