Facts and Figures

  • The UCI Libraries at a Glance
  • Text Version

[Resources]

  • UCI Libraries’ facilities include 350,000 square feet in four locations:
    • Jack Langson Library
    • Science Library
    • Grunigen Medical Library
    • Libraries Gateway Study Center
  • These buildings contain
    • o Over 3,300 public seats
    • o Over 500 public computers
  • The Libraries employ 177 full-time equivalent staff
    • 57 librarians and professionals
    • 97 staff
    • 23 full-time equivalent positions filled by over 165 student employees
  • Annual Expenditures
    • Approximately $22 million

[Collections]

  • The UCI Libraries’ electronic and print collection includes approximately:
    • Over 4 million volumes
    • Over 1.9 million electronic books
    • 253,922 journals and serials
    • 100,814 government documents
    • 113,543 audio/visual materials, multimedia, maps, photographs, and other graphic materials
    • The Libraries Special Collections preserves 640 archival collections that include over 1 million original documents and photographs

[Services]

  • On an annual basis:
    • Librarians answer over 9,000 research and reference questions.
    • Librarians provide research instruction to over 23,000 individuals.
    • UCI Libraries’ buildings receive over 1.6 million visits (averaging to over 4,500 per day).
    • The Libraries’ website receives nearly 3.4 million virtual visits (averaging to over 9,000 per day).
    • UCI Libraries’ facilities are open 19 hours during a typical day and 111 hours during a typical week. Study centers are open 24 hours per day during finals week.
