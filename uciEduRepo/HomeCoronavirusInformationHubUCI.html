Flu Vaccination Requirement for UC Students, Faculty and Staff

The University of California issued a systemwide executive order requiring all students, faculty, other academic appointees and staff to, by Nov. 19, 2021, get vaccinated against influenza or opt out using a form supplied by their location after receiving information about flu vaccine. 

COVID-19 Boosters

Updated CDC guidance recommends boosters for people who received the Pfizer or Moderna vaccines more than six months ago and work in healthcare or education settings and anyone 18 years or older who received J&J two months ago.
