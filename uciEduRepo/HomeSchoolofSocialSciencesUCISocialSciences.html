 from the dean 

 

 

We are not your standard school. Like Peter, our beloved Anteater mascot, were quirky and different. Were on a mission to create positive change in society, economies and human well-being by breaking down traditional barriers and pushing the limits in teaching, research and service. Doing so requires a different attitude, aim and set of actions. Learn more about ours below in our strategic plan.
 
After checking us out and you find youre someone who gets as excited as we do about these possibilities, we invite you to join us in our pursuit.
 
Bill Maurer, Dean
