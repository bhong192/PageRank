Landford Wilson’s “Burn This”
Production available for viewing November 29-December 10, 2021
https://youtu.be/jw-pz6ZX824

Taking place in NYC in the 1980’s, this play is a piece that speaks volumes to the world we’re currently living in now. Set in 1987, at the height of the AIDS epidemic, much of it speaks to the uncertain, unsettling, grief that we are currently facing today. Not being able to articulate the sudden loss of someone, is at the heart beat of the play. In this uncertainty lives the possibility for growth, healing, and love; something we all are in desperate need of today.

Featuring actors Nick Adams, Hope Andrejack, Abel Garcia, & Evan Lugo. Lighting design by Nita Mendoza. Sound design by Josh Price and Garrett Gagnon.
