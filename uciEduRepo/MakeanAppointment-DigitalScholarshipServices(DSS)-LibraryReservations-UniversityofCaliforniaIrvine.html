Make an Appointment -

Make an appointment in 3 steps. Step 1: Select a staff member. Step 2: Select an available date. Step 3: Select an available timeslot.

UCI Libraries' Digital Scholarship Services support digital scholarship work performed by the UCI community, including e-Research, data curation, digital humanities, digital preservation, and Geographical Information Systems (GIS). We offer a variety of services and tools to assist throughout the research lifecycle, from planning and implementing a research project to publishing, preserving, and increasing the visibility and impact of research outcome. Interested in a particular service or tool, or have a question and don't know where to start?

Appointments will be via zoom.  Once you set an appointment a zoom link will be sent to you via email.
