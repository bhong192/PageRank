UCI Graduate Division Mission Statement

Graduate education is what differentiates a university from a college.
At UCI Graduate Division, we strive to:

  1. Recruit the best students to UCI and provide them a high-quality experience and education;
  2. Provide the graduate-level educational resources they need before, during, and after their time
    at UCI;
  3. Help them launch their careers, whether it be in academia, industry, entrepreneurship, non-profit, or government.

To do this, we must excel at diversity, equity, and inclusion, student success and wellness, lifelong learning, and partnership with industry.
