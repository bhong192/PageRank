Event Services & Amenities

Recognized as an international center of excellence, the UC Irvine Conference Center is a world-class venue committed to delivering the highest level of service possible.

Our event specialists and Certified Meeting Professionals® — a credential recognized globally as the badge of excellence in the meeting, convention, exhibition and event industry — are knowledgeable in effective room layouts, business services, conference technology and equipment. They are supported by our dedicated team of audiovisual specialists and Certified Technology Specialists® trained to ensure your event goes according to plan. We measure our success by yours and can be called upon for assistance at any time throughout the planning process and during your event for the best guest experience possible.

  •  Complimentary Wi-Fi

    Free Wi-Fi is available throughout The Center enabling quick and easy access to the Internet.

  •  VIP Guest Services

    Our Event Team is on call should you need to make adjustments to your event from temperature to chairs, the VIP Guest Services line is available to you.

  •  Certified Meeting Planners (CMP®)

    Our CMPs® have completed the rigorous qualification process to make sure that your event occurs as you planned it, covering all the information you need in the pre-planning, production, and post-planning process.

    About the CMP Certification

  •  Certified Technology Specialists (CTS®)

    The event team is supported by technology experts certified and knowledgeable in different aspects of technical enhancement including lighting, sound, projection, and videos.

    About the CTS Certification

  •  Business Center

    Need to print a presentation or a boarding pass, come to the Business Center for all your presentation needs.

    Business Center

  •  Dining Experience

    Whether you are looking for a full dining experience or a convenient, quick fix, we have options suited to your needs.

    Dining

  •  Shuttle Service

    Shuttle services can be arranged between campus and local destinations including John Wayne Airport, off-campus dining, hotel accommodations and other points of interest. Please talk to your event planner. Additional charges will apply.

  •  Integrated Audiovisual Technology and Support

    Most rooms have built-in AV with projectors and screens, VGA connections and HDMI support (by request), audio enhancement, and microphone support.

  •  Accessible Venues The Center is ADA Compliant

    Our goal is to make every meeting, conference, and event accessible with assistive interior design and listening technology.

  •  AntMedia Videography & Photography

    Our in-house videography and photography team can put your next event into focus with imagination and artistry.

    About AntMedia
