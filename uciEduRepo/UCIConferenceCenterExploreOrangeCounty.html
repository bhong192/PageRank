Explore Orange County

Orange County is a world-famous destination known for its surf, its sun, and year-round fun. UCI puts you at the center of it all. With 42 miles of coveted coast, running-biking-hiking trails, legendary theme parks, and world-class shopping, there is always something to do.

Decompress in Aldrich park, a quiet 19-acre botanical garden in the heart of the UCI community or hit the links at the Rancho San Joaquin Golf Course. Spend a day at the Disneyland Park and Resort or relive the wild west at Knott’s Berry Farm amusement park. Venture out on any one of Orange County’s scenic hiking and biking trails or head to the mountains with your snowboard and skis. Whatever is on your agenda, you can energize your team in this one-of-a-kind place.
