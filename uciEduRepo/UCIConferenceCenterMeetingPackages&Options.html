Meeting Packages & Options

Winter Wonderland Year-End Event Package

For holiday events occurring in December 2019. Based on availability

Enjoy themed décor and seasonal menu* with a complete, start-to-finish wonderland escape unlike anything in Orange County.

Package Includes:

  • Certified Meeting Professional® support throughout the planning process
  • Integrated audiovisual package and Certified Technology Specialist® support
  • Your choice of round banquet or cocktail reception setup
  • One-of-a-kind Winter Wonderland décor
  • Full-service catering options*
  • Complimentary photo booth architectural showpiece

For more information, email reserver@uci.edu or call 949.824.5252

*Additional fees apply.

Blue & Gold Event Package

For Commencement Events Between June 15 – 18, 2017

The UCI Conference Center invites graduands, graduates, their families and friends to celebrate commencement in traditional UCI blue and gold decor.

Package Includes:

  • Pacific Ballroom AB (reception), Pacific Ballroom CD (theater) or Doheny Beach (rounds) venue options
  • Pre-event planning assistance
  • Integrated audiovisual package and Certified Technology Specialist® support
  • Your choice of round banquet or cocktail reception setup
  • UCI Blue & Gold décor
  • Full-service catering options*

For more information, email reserver@uci.edu or call 949.824.5252


*Additional fees apply.

Summer Conferences

For Events Between the End of June – End of August

On-campus accommodations and beautiful parks make UCI the perfect choice for summer camps and youth groups — all within walking distance to meeting facilities.

Package Includes:

  • Certified Meeting Professional® support
  • Guest room accommodations
    • One workspace per bed
    • Linen and towel service
    • Shared bathrooms and common areas
  • Dedicated operational support staff

For more information, email us at scesconf@exchange.uci.edu.

  • Crystal Cove Auditorium—425 theater seating with stage
  • House and stage wash lights
  • Professional AV technician
