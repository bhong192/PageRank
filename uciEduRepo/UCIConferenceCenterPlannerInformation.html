 Forms & Permits

Looking for a form or permit? Start here.

  • Sound Permit

    Submit a request to have amplified sound at your outdoor event.

     Request for Sound Form

  • Alcohol Permit

    Submit a request to have alcohol service at your event.

     Tip: Request form must be submitted at least 20 business days in advance of event.

     Request to Serve or Sell
    Alcoholic Beverages

  • Film & Photography Permit

    Submit a request to film or photograph on campus property.

     Application for the UCI
    Film and Photography Permit      Application for the UCI
    Student Film and Photography Permit

  • Vendors

    Vendors who are interested in selling merchandise or providing services on campus should fill out and submit these forms.

     Temporary Vendor Permit  Vendor Agreement

  • Free-Standing, Temporary Structures

    Submit a request to display a free-standing, temporary structure on campus property.

     Tip: Request form must be submitted at least 15 days in advance of event.

     Request for Free-Standing, Temporary Structure

  • Summer Conferences

    Find the forms you need to hold a summer conference on the UCI campus.

    Summer Conference Inquiry Form

  • Storage Waiver

    If you plan to store equipment and personal belongings onsite at the UCI Conference Center overnight, you will need to fill out this form.

     Short-Term, Temporary Custody or Storage of Property

  • Money Deposit Form

    If you are holding a special event that collects funds from participants, you may request to have our onsite staff securely store your collection.

     Money Deposit Form

  • Digital Signage Request Form

    If you would like to have an image promoting your event playing in the UCI Conference Center indoor LCD screens, you may make a request with this form.

     Digital Signage Request Form

  • Appeal Request

    An individual or organization who receives an infraction may appeal in writing within five days of receiving notification of loss of privileges.

     Appeal Request

  • Additional Approval for Catered Events (University Policy BUS-79)

    University business meetings or events associated with a UC campus with catering may require a Signature of Approving Authority as Additional Approval. Submit this completed form to your SCES Planner.

     Additional Approval for Catered Events
