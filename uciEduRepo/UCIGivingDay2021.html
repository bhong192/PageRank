UCI Giving Day 2021

On April 28, 2021, UCI alumni, students, faculty, staff, emeriti, retirees, parents and friends from around the world made a tremendous impact on our campus and community. Thank you to everyone who supported scholarships, groundbreaking research, medical services and innovative programs at UCI. 

While Giving Day 2021 has come to an end, the impact of your generosity will continue on.
