UCI Libraries' Events

By making a reservation and/or attending an event at the UCI Libraries, I hereby grant the UCI Libraries and anyone authorized by the UCI Libraries the unrestricted right to copyright, reproduce, publish and otherwise use my picture, video, audio or power point presentation on the date of the event. I understand that the Libraries seek only to use these items for non-commercial purposes including, but not limited to advertising and promoting the event and the UCI Libraries. This includes displays on the UCI Libraries' website. I hereby waive any causes of action I may have on account of the use of my photograph, video, audio or my presentation made at the Libraries. I acknowledge that I have NOT received any financial compensation and that no such compensation is required for the UCI Libraries to use these items in the above mentioned manner. I represent that I have read the foregoing and fully and completely understand the contents hereof. Any concerns can be addressed to the registration staff at the time of event or in an email to partners@uci.edu, we will do our best to accommodate. Join the UCI Libraries Special Events Mailing List
