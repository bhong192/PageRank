UCI is proud to announce that over the past 20 years we have been a Top Producer for the Gilman Program. We believe that accessibility is important in the world of study abroad and are honored to have been working towards that over the past two decades.

 

This award provides up to $5,000 for U.S. citizen undergraduates to study or intern abroad. Click here for more information on the Benjamin A. Gilman Scholarship.

 

If you are a Federal Pell Grant recipient interested in studying or interning abroad, get in touch or stop by our office to learn more!
