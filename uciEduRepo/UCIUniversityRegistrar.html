Registrar window service will resume on September 1.

For assistance, contact us at registrar@uci.edu,
by phone at (949) 824-6124,
or visit our office from 9 a.m.-5 p.m., M-F.

 

Notices and Upcoming Deadlines:

Students:

Thursday & Friday, November 25 & 26: Thanksgiving Holiday; campus offices closed.

Wednesday, December 15, 5 p.m.: Winter 2022 registration fees due to the Central Cashier.

Friday, December 17, 8 p.m.: Fall 2021 final grades available on StudentAccess.

Monday, December 20: Fall 2021 final grades available on official transcripts.

December 23–January 2: Winter Administrative Recess; campus offices closed.

Faculty/Staff:

Friday, December 3, 5 p.m.: Begin submitting final grades for Fall 2021 via WebGrades.

Thursday, December 16, 5 p.m.: Deadline to submit final grades for Fall 2021 via WebGrades.


Who We Are:

Registrar (rej • ? • strär)
noun
an official in a college or university who is responsible for maintaining student records, issuing reports of grades, distributing official publications, etc.

The University Registrar aims to provide academic support services in an atmosphere of prompt, accurate, and friendly service to the campus community.
