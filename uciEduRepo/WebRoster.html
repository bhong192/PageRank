Click here to authenticate yourself so you can access this program.

Note that you must reauthorize yourself whenever you close all of your web browser windows.

Note: If the authentication website has just now accepted your password, but returned you to the same web page here that you saw before you entered your password, then it's possible that:
  1. Your browser may only work with WebAuth in "https://" mode. Try accessing the program here:
    https://www.reg.uci.edu/perl/WebRoster
  2. Cookies may be disabled on your web browser.
    If this is the case, please enable them and log in again so that NACS' WebAuth program can store your authentication cookie for access by this program.

  3. Your web browser settings may cause your browser to store a copy of this page in its cache directory on your hard drive, without bothering to contact our server to load a newer page.

    Check for this by clicking your browser's Reload button.

    If the program then responds correctly, you need to change your web browser's caching preferences.
